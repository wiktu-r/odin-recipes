Creamy Mushroom Risotto

Description

If you're a fan of rich and creamy risotto, this recipe is a must-try. This mushroom risotto is made with Arborio rice cooked to perfection in a flavorful mushroom broth. The creamy texture and earthy flavors make it a comforting and delicious dish that is sure to impress. Follow the steps below to create a restaurant-quality mushroom risotto at home.

Ingredients

  • 4 cups mushroom broth
  • 1 cup Arborio rice
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • 1/2 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 8 ounces mushrooms, sliced
  • 1/4 cup white wine
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh parsley
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. In a saucepan, heat the mushroom broth over medium heat until warm. Keep it on low heat while preparing the risotto.
  2. In a separate large saucepan, melt the butter and olive oil over medium heat. Add the chopped onion and minced garlic, and saut√© until they become translucent.
  3. Add the sliced mushrooms to the saucepan and cook until they are tender and browned. Remove a few mushroom slices for garnishing later, if desired.
  4. Add the Arborio rice to the saucepan and stir well to coat the grains with the butter and oil mixture. Cook for a minute to lightly toast the rice.
  5. Pour in the white wine and stir until it is absorbed by the rice.
  6. Ladle in a small amount of warm mushroom broth (about 1/2 cup) to the rice mixture. Stir constantly until the broth is absorbed. Repeat this process, adding more broth gradually as it is absorbed, until the rice is al dente (tender with a slight bite) and creamy in texture. This will take about 20-25 minutes.
  7. Stir in the grated Parmesan cheese and chopped fresh parsley. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Remove from heat.
  8. Cover the saucepan and let the risotto rest for a couple of minutes to allow the flavors to meld.
  9. Serve the mushroom risotto in bowls, garnished with the reserved mushroom slices and an additional sprinkle of Parmesan cheese and parsley, if desired.
